Space Technology in 2026

Humans are going back to space and beyond!

Space Exploration in 2026

Space technology has improved so much in recent years. In 2026, private companies like SpaceX and Blue Origin are sending rockets to space every week. Going to space is becoming cheaper and more common. Some people even pay to go to space as tourists!

Big Space Events of 2026

Moon Mission

NASA's Artemis program is planning to send humans back to the Moon. Astronauts are preparing to build a small base on the Moon where they can live and do experiments.

Mars Exploration

Robots and rovers are still exploring Mars in 2026. Scientists are learning about the surface of Mars to prepare for when humans might visit in the future.

Space Tourism

In 2026, rich people can pay to go on a trip to space. The trip goes around the Earth and they can see our planet from space. A ticket costs millions of dollars but the price is going down.

Satellites and GPS

Did you know that when you use GPS on your phone, you are using satellites in space? In 2026, there are thousands of satellites orbiting the Earth. They help with GPS navigation, internet connection, weather forecasting, and military uses.

Interesting Satellite Fact

SpaceX's Starlink project has launched thousands of small satellites to provide internet to every corner of the Earth. Even people in the middle of the ocean or in remote jungles can now get internet because of this!
