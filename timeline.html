Technology Timeline

How technology changed the world from 1990 to 2026

The History of Modern Technology

Technology has changed so much in the last 35 years. Here is a timeline of the most important technology events that led to where we are today in 2026!

1991

The World Wide Web is Invented

Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web. This made the internet easy to use for normal people. Before this, only scientists and universities used the internet.

1998

Google is Founded

Google was started by two students from Stanford University. It became the most popular way to search for information on the internet and changed how people used the web.

2004

Facebook is Created

Social media started becoming very popular with Facebook. This changed how people communicated and shared information with each other around the world.

2007

First iPhone Released

Apple released the first iPhone and changed everything. Smartphones became the most important technology device for almost every person on Earth.

2010

iPad and Tablet Computers

Apple released the iPad and tablet computers became popular. This gave people a new way to use the internet and read books, watch videos, and play games.

2012

Rise of Cloud Computing

Cloud services like Google Drive and Dropbox became popular. People started storing their files on the internet instead of on their computers.

2016

VR Headsets Go on Sale

The first consumer VR headsets like the Oculus Rift went on sale. Virtual Reality started becoming something normal people could buy and use at home.

2020

5G Internet Launches

5G internet started rolling out around the world. This was 100 times faster than 4G and allowed new technologies like self-driving cars to work better.

2022

ChatGPT Released to the Public

OpenAI released ChatGPT which was an AI that anyone could talk to and get help from. It became the fastest growing app in history and changed how people work and learn.

2023

AI Revolution Begins

AI tools started being used by millions of people every day. AI could write, draw pictures, make music, and write code. Many jobs started changing because of AI.

2024

First Mass-Market AI Glasses

The first popular AI smart glasses were released. People could wear glasses that could translate languages, identify objects, and give them information about what they see.

2025

Humanoid Robots Enter Workplaces

Tesla's Optimus and other humanoid robots started working in real factories and warehouses. This was the first time human-shaped robots were used at large scale in real workplaces.

2026

6G Testing and AI Everywhere

6G internet testing is successful. AI is now used in almost every job and industry. Flying taxis are operating in some cities. And technology is changing faster than ever before!

What Comes Next?

Nobody knows exactly what technology will bring next. But here are some things scientists are working on that might happen soon:

  • Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) - AI smarter than humans
  • Human bases on the Moon by 2028-2030
  • Human missions to Mars by 2030-2035
  • Nuclear Fusion energy becoming available
  • Brain-computer interfaces that let humans control computers with their minds
