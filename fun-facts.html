Did You Know These Tech Facts?

Technology is so amazing that it is hard to believe some of these facts are real! Read through these and share with your friends!

1 Every single day, more than 5 billion searches are made on Google. That is more searches than there are people on Earth!
2 The average smartphone in 2026 has more computing power than all of NASA's computers combined when they first sent astronauts to the Moon in 1969!
3 If you started counting every website on the internet at 1 per second, it would take you more than 600 years to count them all!
4 The first computer mouse was made of wood! It was invented by Douglas Engelbart in 1964 and was a simple wooden box with a single button.
5 YouTube users upload more than 500 hours of video every single minute. That means while you read this, thousands of videos were uploaded!
6 The first text message ever sent was on December 3rd 1992. It said "Merry Christmas" and was sent by a 22-year-old engineer in the UK.
7 Bitcoin was invented by a mysterious person (or group) known only as Satoshi Nakamoto. Nobody knows who this person really is - it is one of the biggest mysteries in technology!
8 The average person in 2026 spends about 7 hours per day looking at screens. That is almost one third of the entire day!
9 There are now more active SIM cards in the world than there are people. Many people have more than one phone or SIM card!
10 The first ever email was sent in 1971. The person who sent it, Ray Tomlinson, cannot remember what the message said. It was probably just test letters like "QWERTYUIOP"!
11 NVIDIA, the company that makes AI chips, went from being worth $300 billion in 2022 to over $3 trillion in 2024 because of the AI boom. That is 10 times bigger in just 2 years!
12 An AI called AlphaFold solved one of the biggest mysteries in science - how proteins fold. Scientists had been trying to solve this for 50 years and AI solved it in months!
