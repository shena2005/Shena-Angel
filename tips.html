Tech Tips for 2026

Helpful tips to use technology better and safer

Simple Tech Tips Everyone Should Know

Here are some very useful tips to help you use technology better in 2026. These tips can help you stay safe, save time, and get more out of your devices.

Phone Tips

Make Your Phone Last Longer

  • Do not charge your phone to 100% all the time - keep it between 20% and 80% for best battery life
  • Turn off WiFi and Bluetooth when you are not using them to save battery
  • Lower your screen brightness - this is one of the biggest battery drains
  • Close apps you are not using so they do not run in the background
  • Keep your phone updated because updates often fix bugs that drain battery

Internet Safety Tips

Stay Safe Online

  • Use a different password for every important account
  • Use a password manager app to remember all your passwords
  • Turn on 2-factor authentication on all important accounts
  • Never click on links in emails from people you do not know
  • Be careful what you download - only download from official app stores
  • Use a VPN when you use public WiFi in cafes, airports etc
  • Never share your passwords with anyone, even friends

AI Tips

Using AI Chatbots Better

When asking AI chatbots questions, be very specific and detailed. Instead of asking "help me write an email" say "help me write a professional email to my teacher asking for an extension on my project because I was sick."

Always Check AI Answers

AI can make mistakes and sometimes gives wrong information confidently. Always double check important information from AI using a second source like a book or official website.

Protect Your Privacy with AI

Do not share personal information with AI chatbots like your home address, phone number, credit card numbers, or passwords. AI conversations might be saved and read by people.

Computer Speed Tips

  • Restart your computer at least once a week to clear memory
  • Delete files and programs you do not need to free up space
  • Keep your operating system and programs updated
  • Use a good antivirus program to prevent viruses
  • If your computer is slow, adding more RAM memory is often the best upgrade
"The best technology is technology you understand how to use properly. Take time to learn your devices and they will serve you much better!"
