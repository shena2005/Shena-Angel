The companies that are shaping our world

The Biggest Technology Companies

Some technology companies are so big and powerful that they affect the lives of almost every person on Earth. In 2026, these are the most important and valuable technology companies in the world.

Top Tech Companies 2026

Apple

Apple makes the iPhone, iPad, Mac computers, and Apple Watch. In 2026 they are also working on AI features and smart glasses. Apple is known for making products that are very easy and nice to use.

Microsoft

Microsoft makes Windows, Office, and Xbox games. In 2026 they are also a very big player in AI because they heavily invested in OpenAI which makes ChatGPT. They are also a big cloud computing company.

Google / Alphabet

Google runs the most popular search engine and makes Android phones. They also make their own AI called Gemini and have many other products including YouTube, Google Maps, and Google Cloud.


Amazon

Amazon started as an online shop but now they also run the biggest cloud computing service called AWS. They also sell Alexa smart speakers, Kindle e-readers, and delivery robots.

NVIDIA

NVIDIA makes the graphics chips (GPUs) that are used to train AI. In 2026 they are one of the most valuable companies in the world because everyone needs their chips to run AI.

Tesla / SpaceX

Tesla makes electric cars and AI-powered self-driving technology. SpaceX makes rockets. Both companies are run by the same person and are changing transportation on Earth and in space.
