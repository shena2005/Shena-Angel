Blockchain Technology

The technology behind Bitcoin and Web3

What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a special way of storing information on many computers at the same time. Instead of one company keeping a database, thousands of computers all share the same data. This makes it very hard to cheat or change the information because everyone has a copy.

Think of it like a notebook that millions of people have an exact copy of. If someone tries to change one page in their copy, everyone else's copy still shows the original. So the cheat would be caught right away!

What is Blockchain Used For?

Cryptocurrency

Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other cryptocurrencies use blockchain to keep track of who owns what money. There is no bank needed because the blockchain keeps records automatically.

NFTs

NFT means Non-Fungible Token. It is a way to prove that you own a digital item like an image or video. In 2026, NFTs are used in gaming and digital art.

Smart Contracts

A smart contract is a program on the blockchain that automatically does something when conditions are met. For example, automatically paying someone when a job is done, without needing a bank or lawyer.

Is Cryptocurrency Real Money?

Cryptocurrency is real money that many people use. In 2026, you can buy things with Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies in many shops and online. However it is also very risky because the value can go up or down a lot very fast.

Popular Cryptocurrencies in 2026

  • Bitcoin (BTC) - The first and most famous cryptocurrency
  • Ethereum (ETH) - Used for smart contracts and NFTs
  • Solana (SOL) - A fast and cheap blockchain
  • USDC - A stable cryptocurrency always worth $1 USD
